## How to cite

Please cite the archived release of this project (DOI to be issued via
Zenodo). See `CITATION.cff` for the recommended citation format.

When using individual TEI transcriptions, OpenAtlas entries, or derived
data, please also cite the upstream critical edition or manuscript source
recorded in the relevant entry, where applicable. For French royal ordines,
the foundational edition is:

> Jackson, Richard A., ed. *Ordines Coronationis Franciae: Texts and
> Ordines for the Coronation of Frankish and French Kings and Queens in
> the Middle Ages*. 2 vols. Philadelphia: University of Pennsylvania
> Press, 1995–2000.
